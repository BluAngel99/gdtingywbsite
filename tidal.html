Tidal Wave

Tidal Wave is a 2.1 solo Extreme Demon level created and published by OniLink and verified by Zoink on 9 September 2023 after 49,534 attempts. Tidal Wave was rated and placed at #1 on the Demonlist on 18 February 2024, over five months after its verification, surpassing Avernus and becoming the current hardest level in Geometry Dash.
